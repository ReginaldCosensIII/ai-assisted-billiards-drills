## Status Definitions
- **Accepted**: Decision is approved and binding for the current scope.
- **Pending**: Decision is required but has not yet been made. Work that depends on it may be blocked.
- **Deferred**: Decision is intentionally not being made in the current phase.
- **Superseded**: A later ADR has replaced this decision.
